# Stage 3 Concept: The Infinite IKEA-pocalypse 🇸🇪📦

*"You've entered a sentient, endless furniture warehouse. There is no exit. There is only the Meatball."*

---

## Overview

**Stage Name:** The Infinite IKEA-pocalypse

**Theme:** A surreal, liminal space that looks like a Swedish furniture store designed by M.C. Escher.

**Difficulty Multiplier:** 1.8x (The mental toll of the layout is the real challenge!)

---

## Visual Style

- **Color Palette:** Bright yellow and blue (Swedish flag colors)
- **Road:** Giant conveyor belt with white floors
- **Background:** Endless rows of showrooms, floating Swedish flags
- **Atmosphere:** Liminal space anxiety meets quirky Scandinavian design
- **Sky:** Fluorescent ceiling panels stretching into infinity

---

## Obstacles (5-10 Ideas)

1. **Rogue Meatballs** 🍖 — Giant, bouncing meatballs that try to flatten you
2. **The "Assembly Required" Pile** 📐 — Mountain of wooden planks and screws to navigate
3. **Slippery Floor Signs** ⚠️ — Wet floor signs that actually make you slide uncontrollably
4. **Confused Shoppers** 🛒 — NPCs pushing giant carts that block the path
5. **Floating Allen Keys** 🔧 — Spinning hazards that act like buzz-saws
6. **Wardrobe Portals** 🚪 — Jump into one wardrobe, pop out of another randomly
7. **Bookshelf Towers** 📚 — Unstable stacks that wobble and fall
8. **Price Tag Banners** 🏷️ — Hanging labels you must duck under
9. **The Return Counter** — A barrier that tries to send you backward
10. **Flat-Pack Boxes** 📦 — Collapsible obstacles that expand when you approach

---

## Boss: The Flat-Pack Behemoth 📦

**Description:** A towering monster made of mismatched bookshelves, sofas, and lamps.

**Attack Patterns:**
- Constantly "re-assembles" itself to change attack patterns
- Throws instruction manuals that confuse controls
- Drops furniture pieces from above
- Opens drawers that shoot out socks and screws

**Defeat Strategy:** Dodge the assembly attacks, wait for the "instruction phase" when it's vulnerable, then strike!

---

## Special Mechanics

### Instruction Manual Glitch
Every 30 seconds, the "instructions" change, causing screen effects:
- **Flip:** Screen turns upside down
- **Rotate:** Screen rotates 90 degrees
- **Invert:** Colors invert (negative effect)
- **Mirror:** Everything flips horizontally

### Conveyor Belt Physics
- Road speed varies (sometimes faster, sometimes slower)
- Occasional "reverse" sections where you must fight the belt

### Showroom Shortcuts
- Enter showrooms for bonus coins
- Risk: May contain hidden obstacles
